    • Agile Enablement

    Agile organizations exhibit five key traits: a network of teams within a people-centric culture, rapid learning, quick decision-making, technology facilitation, and a shared purpose that creates value for all stakeholders. These characteristics align with insights from the article on building agile organizations.

    Agile Principles

    Agile enablement principles include using a minimal viable process to keep workflows simple, applying short cycles and fast loops for quick adaptation, adopting lean governance for flexible decision-making, and following agile guiding principles that focus on customer collaboration and iterative progress.

    Dimensions of Enterprise Agility

  • Organization Model: Defines team structures and collaboration methods.
  • Planning and Performance Management: Emphasizes flexible and iterative goal-setting.
  • Technology Enablers: Provides tools and platforms for agile practices.
  • Talent Model: Develops and aligns skills with agile principles.
  • Organization Model: Adopts practices for better collaboration and adaptability.
